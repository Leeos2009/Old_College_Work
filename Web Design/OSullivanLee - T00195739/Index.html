Our product is a Virtual Classroom, which provides a full education to people who are unable to attend school due to unforeseen circumstances with the help of professionally trained teachers.

Our product aims to help people who suffer from some form of disability/mental illness, earn an education from the comfort of their own home using a virtual reality headset.

To use our product, you will need a Virtual Reality Headset, along with an internet connection with an minimum connection strength of NAT Type 2 and 10 megabytes per second.
