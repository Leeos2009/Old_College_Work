Our product is aimed at helping people who are not able to attend school due to unforeseen circumstances, such as disabilities/mental illnesses/location. in the world today there are over 1 billion people who suffer from some form of disability/mental illness and we hope to provide those of them who want to learn a full education.



Our product is still in production, and this is an early prototype image of our Virtual Reality Classroom Design. When you sign up for our product, you will be granted the opportunity to create your own customisable avatar.
